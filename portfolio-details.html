Transform Lives Through Style: Shop handcrafted products and beautiful hair made by survivors at Daughters of Freedom Uganda. Every purchase supports their journey of healing and empowerment.

Empowering Through Craftsmanship

At Daughters of Freedom Uganda, we believe in the power of skills and creativity to transform lives. Our survivors, once victims of trafficking and commercial sexual exploitation, are now empowered artisans, creating a range of handmade products and hair extensions. Every item crafted is a symbol of their resilience, determination, and hope for a brighter future

Why you should Shop With Us

Handcrafted with Love: Each product is meticulously made by survivors who have learned valuable skills in crafting and design, ensuring top-quality items that are as beautiful as they are meaningful.

Support a Cause: Your purchase directly contributes to the rehabilitation and empowerment of survivors, funding education, skills training, and counseling to help them rebuild their lives.

Ethical and Sustainable: Our products are crafted using locally sourced, sustainable materials, supporting the environment and local communities while empowering the women who make them.

Our Products Include:

Handmade Accessories: Discover a variety of beautiful jewelry, bags, and accessories that combine traditional craftsmanship with modern designs.

Hair Extensions: Our natural and high-quality hair extensions are made with care by skilled survivors, ensuring both beauty and quality for our customers.

Home Decor and Gifts: From handwoven baskets to unique home accents, each piece tells a story of resilience and hope.

My name is Amina, and I am a survivor. Before finding Daughters of Freedom Uganda, my life was filled with fear and hopelessness. But today, I stand proud as a skilled artisan, creating beautiful products that tell my story of survival and strength. When you buy from us, you're not just getting a product—you’re supporting women like me on their journey to freedom and independence. Every item we make is crafted with love and hope for a better tomorrow. Your purchase helps us rebuild our lives, learn new skills, and become self-sufficient. Thank you for believing in us and for being a part of our healing and empowerment. Together, we can make a difference!"

Amina, Daughters of Freedom Uganda Survivor
